Privacy Policy – Italy Travel Guide

Italy Travel Guide respects your privacy.

This application is designed to help tourists explore Italy more easily by providing travel information, nearby search functionality, navigation support, metro information, tourist safety tips, emergency numbers, restaurants, and popular places to visit.

Information Collection

Italy Travel Guide does not collect, store, or share personal information such as:

Full name
Address
Email address
Payment information
Personal photos or files
Location Usage

The application may use your device location only to:

Show nearby places
Create navigation routes
Improve travel experience
Open directions using Google Maps

Location access is optional and controlled entirely by the user through device permissions.

Maps & Navigation

This application uses external map services such as Google Maps for navigation features.

An internet connection and a maps application installed on your Android phone or tablet may be required for some navigation features.

Device Compatibility

Italy Travel Guide is designed for Android phones and tablets.

The application has been tested on Android devices and scanned for security and safe installation.

Third-Party Services

The app may open external services including:

Google Maps
Web links
Navigation applications

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

This application is not directed toward children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in future versions of the application.
